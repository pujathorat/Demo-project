About Us

Our IPS school is focusing on right skills with right attitude i.e. positive attitude needs further in higher education. We have low child Teacher ratio. It is situated opposite of Market yard Kopargaon on old Takali road. Special Abacus, Phonics and Competitive exam batches are available in our school. We use Play way Method based on 3-E factors: Energy, Emotion and Empathy based on Indian culture curriculum. Energy improves Self Development in kids. Emotion improves social development in kids. Empathy improves Environmental awareness as well as Patriotism. Our goal is very clear "Seed the Right Values"
