About Us

Welcome to IPS School of Yoga! We are thrilled to invite you to embark on a journey of self-discovery, growth and wellness. Whether you are a beginner looking to explore the practice of yoga for the first time, or a seasoned practitioner seeking to deepen your understanding and skills, our courses are designed to meet your needs and help you achieve your goals.At our Yoga School, you will have the opportunity to explore your hidden potential and discover your true self. Yoga is not just about physical postures, it's a holistic practice that can help you to find balance, peace and inner harmony. By connecting your body, mind and breath, you will learn to release tension, stress and negative emotions, and replace them with feelings of enthusiasm, happiness and energy.
So, join us today and start your yoga journey, whether you are looking to become a practitioner or a teacher. We are dedicated to providing you with the best experience and support, so you can reach your full potential and live your best life.

Our Courses for Kids and Teachers

Meditation "Meditation, which is the practice of focused concentration, bringing yourself back to the moment over and over again, actually addresses stress, whether positive or negative. It also reduce the areas of anxiety, chronic pain, depression, heart disease and high blood pressure.

The Surya Namaskar tones the entire body, helps with weight loss, and strengthens muscles and joints. Practice this flow if you want to improve your complexion as it ensures a better functioning digestive system
